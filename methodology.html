Methodology & Sources

How Lincoln Audit Explorer is built

Our goal

Help Lincoln residents understand the City's audits using plain language while preserving accuracy.

Primary source

The public records is the organized evidence source used for every public statement in this application. It is built from publicly available records and preserves references back to those records.

Public records residents can review

  • Independent audit reports
  • City Council meeting packets
  • Approved meeting minutes
  • Ordinances and resolutions
  • Financial reports
  • Video recordings of City Council meetings

Residents can locate these records through the City of Lincoln's public website and may review the original documents and meeting videos directly.

Facts and interpretation

Documented facts are always separated from supported interpretation so readers can distinguish the public record from explanatory context.

Independence

Lincoln Audit Explorer is an independent public information project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by the City of Lincoln.
